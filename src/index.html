TRAIN

Train accidents have not been foreign in North American. Although the personnel of the railway companies are trained to deal with this type of accidents, these are still occurring and with deadly consequences. Among the causes we have: new routes, taking the wrong lane, speeding or derailment.

PEDALCYCLIST

The bicycle is an accessible, ecological and beneficial means of transport for health. Its use has increased in recent years, but it is also accidental. A study confirms that these accidents become a combination of inadequate infrastructure and risky behavior on the part of drivers and cyclists.

MOTOCYCLIST

Due to the high fuel costs, in recent years the use of motorcycles in the United States has increased, and therefore, accidents in this vehicle have increased. According to the Center for Disease Control, the parts of the body that sustained the most injuries from motorcycle accidents (non-fatal) are: Foot or legs (30%), head / neck (22%). upper part of the trunk (20%), razos or manos (18%) and the lower part of the trunk (8%).

CAR

Deaths in -or by-cars are an urgent public health concern. It is the second cause of accidental death in the country, after drug overdoses. It is a constant struggle with respect to state legislatures since the necessary measures are not taken to stop the pace of deaths

BUS

In the United States, there has been a steady increase in the number of accidents with buses of all kinds over the past 20 years. Among the main causes we have: fatigue of the driver, distracted driver, using some type of electronic device, mechanical failure of some kind or an intoxicated driver, speeding, etc.
